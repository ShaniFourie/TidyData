Introduction

The aim of the study was to assess how disturbance (bait collecting and trampling) influence trophic niches of sandprawns.The study was done in Langebaan lagoon in Zone A. Zone A was further divided into four sections, A1, A2, B and C. To access the raw data used in this document click here.

Within the 4 subsections of Zone A five variables were measured for each of the four materials (Sandprawn, plants, phytoplankton and MPB, and the sediment). These variables are as follows: delta carbon 13, delta nitrogen 15, the % nitrogen present, the % carbon present, and the carbon to nitrogen ratio.
